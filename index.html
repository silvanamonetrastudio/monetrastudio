Who we are

Two cousins. One mission.

We're Diana and Silvana — cousins who turned a shared obsession into a business. Monetra comes from monetize. Studio comes from the belief that building something good is a craft, not a shortcut.

We started this because we kept seeing the same thing: creators with loyal, engaged audiences and nothing to sell them. Not because they weren't talented — but because building a product takes a completely different set of skills than building an audience.

So we made a deal with ourselves. We'd grow by helping others grow. Every product we build for a creator is a product we pour real work into. Their win is our win. That's not a tagline — it's how we keep score.

We're early. We're ambitious. And we're betting that the best way to build something lasting is to make sure the people we work with do too.
